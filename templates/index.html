Welcome to Resume Checker.

Our system analyzes resume content and predicts the probability of getting placed.

  • We help bridge the gap between the company's expectations and the user's capabilities by recommending which skill sets are most important according to the job role.
  • Our system also helps categorize skill sets and highlight them in the resume.
  • Another key benefit of our platform is its ease of use. We have designed our system to be intuitive and user-friendly, allowing even those with limited technical skills to quickly and easily create polished and professional resumes.

Finally, our team is dedicated to providing exceptional customer support to our users. Whether they have questions about our platform, need help with their resumes, or simply want to share feedback or suggestions, our team is always here to help. We believe that this commitment to customer service is essential to building strong relationships with our users and ensuring their success.

Technologies Used

The system, which uses NLP and deep learning techniques, will analyze resume content and predict the probability of getting placed. Different modules of this system will be developed using Python and will incorporate MongoDB, Postgres, TensorFlow, NLTK, Spacy, FastAPI, Docker, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flask, Scrappy, Selenium, and Cloud technologies.

Process

The development process will involve data scraping, data cleaning, database management, API development, NLP, web development, deployment into a cloud service, and CI/CD. Although it may take a long time to develop, this application will be very useful for students and job seekers, and will help them create a robust and relevant resume that is tailored to the specific job requirements.

Aim

This proposed system aims to solve the problem of users being unfamiliar with the skill sets and projects required for a particular job, and not knowing what needs to be included in their resume to meet job demands.

Primary Focus

The primary focus will be on data roles, and the system will bridge the gap between the company's expectations and the user's capabilities by recommending which skill sets are most important according to the job role. It will also help categorize skill sets and highlight them in the resume.
